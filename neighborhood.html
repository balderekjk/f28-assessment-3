Neighborhood

I live in the vicinity of Waiehu. It is "makai", close to the ocean. Some people like to surf at a spot called "Churches". Many longtime local residents live in this area as well as many young families. There are some small family-run stores nearby. Some closed business due to COVID economy hit, but now they are coming back.

Restaurants

Blazin Steaks

Fulfills the steak craving at a very affordable price. Simple and appropriate seasoning.

Keyakiya

Right outside of Waiehu, early into Kahului Beach Road, nearing the harbor entrance. The best Katsu maybe in all of Hawaii among other amazing chicken/pork plates. Natural ingredients.

Parks

Waiehu Terrace Park

Largest neighborhood park in Waiehu, nice trail loop and basketball court.

Waiehu Golf Course

Peaceful golf course of Waiehu.
